Role Responsibilities

How responsibilities are divided across the delivery team.

Mikey

Lead Tutor

  • Delivers 6 live qualification sessions (2 hrs each)
  • Produces all pre-recorded content
  • Oversees programme quality and structure
  • Presents programme to management and EQA
  • No involvement in standardisation or learner allocation

Katja

Assessments Manager

  • Allocates two learners per trainee assessor (one Level 2, one Level 3)
  • Runs monthly standardisation sessions (90 minutes)
  • Countersigns all trainee assessor decisions
  • Maintains the trainee assessor tracking log
  • Acts as gatekeeper before any learner work is touched

Simi and Enrolment Team

Management and Intake

  • Manages CAVA programme enrolment and sales
  • Issues learner consent and upgrade form
  • Shares learner name and email address only (GDPR compliant)
  • Passes confirmed consenting learners to Katja for allocation

Trainee Assessor

Programme Participant

  • Completes Unit 1 written questions before standardisation
  • Attends standardisation before accessing any learner work
  • Assesses two allocated learners under countersignature
  • Builds a portfolio of evidence across all three units
  • Submits completed portfolio for IQA sampling

Learner Upgrade Model

How PT Academy self-study learners become practice learners for trainee assessors.

Step 1

Consent

Simi's team contacts eligible self-study learners and issues the consent and upgrade form. Only name and email shared.

Step 2

Allocation

Katja receives confirmed consenting learners and assigns one Level 2 and one Level 3 to each trainee assessor.

Step 3

Upgrade Benefit

Learner receives a trainee assessor as a goodwill gesture. All decisions are countersigned by Katja as the qualified assessor.

Standardisation Model

Run by Katja monthly. Trainee assessors must complete this before touching any learner work.

How Latecomers Are Managed

The Gate Rule

No trainee assessor may access or assess any learner work until they have completed a standardisation session with Katja.

  • Standardisation runs once per month
  • Trainee assessors who join between sessions complete Unit 1 written questions while they wait
  • A pre-recorded standardisation overview is available on the platform to watch in advance
  • The live session with Katja then serves as calibration and confirmation rather than a full delivery
