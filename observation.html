Purpose of this Observation:

This observation record documents the assessment of a trainee assessor delivering vocational assessment to learners. The observation logs the protocol of assessment delivery, including how the trainee assessor engages with learners, manages assessment activities, and provides feedback. This observation may be carried out on more than one occasion to gather comprehensive evidence of occupational competence in vocational assessment delivery.
