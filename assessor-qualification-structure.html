6

Post-Week 6

Quality Assurance Gates

Standardisation (Week 1)

Katja and team run calibration meeting to establish consistent assessment practice and evidence quality standards.

Gate: Trainees must demonstrate understanding of VARCS, assessment methods and evidence quality judgements before proceeding to learner allocation.

Countersigning (Weeks 3–5)

Katja countersigns all trainee assessment decisions and evidence quality judgements.

Gate: Ensures consistency and prevents assessment errors before trainee evidence is finalised.

IQA Sampling (Week 6)

Jodie and Charlotte review trainee portfolios and assessment decisions to verify quality and compliance.

Gate: IQA must sign off portfolio as fit for submission before external review. Any gaps addressed before EQA.

External Quality Assurance (EQA)

Focus Awards conduct external review of trainee portfolios and assessment practice standards.

Gate: EQA confirms trainee has met all qualification requirements and is competent as an assessor.
