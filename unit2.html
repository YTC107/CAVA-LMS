Learning Outcome 1 • Be able to plan the assessment of occupational competence

Assessment Criteria 1.1 • 1.2 • 1.3 • 1.4
What this Learning Outcome is about: This outcome requires you to show that you can plan assessments effectively for learners working towards occupational qualifications. You must demonstrate that you are able to select appropriate assessment methods, communicate clearly with each learner, adapt your planning to suit individual needs, and recognise where assessment activity can cover more than one criterion. Evidence must relate to Learner 1 (Level 2 Gym Instructor) and Learner 2 (Level 3 Personal Training).

Learning Outcome 2 • Be able to make assessment decisions about occupational competence

Assessment Criteria 2.1 • 2.2 • 2.3 • 2.4
What this Learning Outcome is about: This outcome requires you to demonstrate that you can make sound, well-reasoned assessment decisions about learner competence. You must show that you use valid, fair and reliable assessment methods, that your decisions are made against specified criteria, that you follow your organisation's standardisation procedures, and that you provide meaningful feedback to learners. Evidence must relate to Learner 1 (Level 2 Gym Instructor) and Learner 2 (Level 3 Personal Training).
Guidance for AC 2.1: You must demonstrate use of all seven assessment methods across both learners. Learner 1 evidence must come from the Level 2 Gym Instructor programme. Learner 2 evidence must come from the Level 3 Personal Training programme.
